Autonomous Robot (Engineering Physics Robot Course, UBC)

May - Aug 2022

In this course, we were given approximately 8 weeks to build an autonomous robot in a team of four to compete in a competition consisting of overcoming challenging obstacles. I engaged in mechanical design and prototyping in addition to building parts of the electrical system.
